# Session Reporting - Perimeter Explorer

## Report Goal
The child experiences a running game; the teacher sees a structured perimeter worksheet with evidence of learning.

## Data Fields
For each question log: level, question number, shape type, unit, prompt text, visible side lengths, inferred side lengths, correct answer, child answer, attempt count, time taken, whether phantom was used, and curriculum code.

## Question Card Diagram
Show the shape/track with outer boundary highlighted, side labels visible, and missing side variable where relevant. Do not show decorative park background in report diagrams unless it helps identify the task.

## Summary
Include total questions, correct first try, corrected after retry, accuracy, total time, level completion, and final shoe progress.

## Curriculum Statement
`Perimeter Explorer practises NSW Mathematics K-10 Stage 3 outcome MA3-GM-02: selecting and using appropriate units/devices to measure lengths and distances including perimeters.`
