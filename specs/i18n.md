# i18n - Perimeter Explorer

## Languages
English built in. Chinese and Hindi on demand through the platform translation API.

## Translation Keys
- `game.title`: Perimeter Explorer
- `level.1.name`: Trail Tracer
- `level.2.name`: Track Builder
- `level.3.name`: Coach's Clipboard
- `prompt.traceLap`: Trace the lap.
- `prompt.findPerimeter`: What is the perimeter?
- `prompt.findMissingSide`: Find the missing side.
- `feedback.correct`: Great lap!
- `feedback.wrongShort`: Not quite enough distance. Try again.
- `feedback.wrongLong`: A little too far. Try again.
- `report.curriculum`: NSW Stage 3 Mathematics MA3-GM-02

## Prompt Substitution
Use placeholder substitution for `{shape}`, `{perimeter}`, `{side}`, `{unit}`, `{value}`, and `{variable}`.

## Notes
Keep unit symbols (`cm`, `m`, `km`) untranslated unless the platform language policy says otherwise.
